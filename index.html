Welcome to Choco-Lava® - Where Flavor & Joy Meet!

Enjoy golden, freshly made waffles & rich, aromatic coffee, crafted just for you. Every bite is packed with incredible flavors & memories.

Our story began with a love of chocolate.

At Choco Lava, we're not just a sweets shop, we're a place that brings together all lovers of happiness in one dish. Our idea started from our passion to offer a different experience, a place where when you enter you smell the aroma of fresh waffles and see waterfalls of chocolate cascading over hot dumplings. Our goal is simple: to transform your ordinary day into an exceptional moment with every bite of waffle or cup of heart-warming chocolate.
